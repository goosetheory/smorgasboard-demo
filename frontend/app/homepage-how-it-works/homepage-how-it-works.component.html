How it works

1

Show your board

Connect your laptop to a TV or projector and log in to our website.

2

Guests add photos

Anyone can visit a link or scan your board's QR code to add a photo. When they do, it'll appear instantly. No sign in or app download necessary.

3

Have fun!

As people add photos, SmorgasBoard shows them off in an evolving collage. Afterwards, we'll send you a digital album.
